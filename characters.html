Meet the Characters of Hyrule

Discover my top six favorite heroes and foes of Breath of the Wild

Link

The hero of Hyrule, Link embarks on a journey to defeat Calamity Ganon.

Zelda

The princess of Hyrule, burdened with the task of sealing away Ganon.

Revali

A skilled Rito warrior with unmatched archery skills.

Hestu

A giant Korok with a love for dancing and maracas. He helps Link expand his inventory in exchange for Korok Seeds.

Impa

The elder of Kakariko Village and a key figure in the Sheikah tribe. She provides Link with guidance on his quest to defeat Calamity Ganon.

Sidon

The enthusiastic and courageous prince of the Zora. He assists Link in the battle against Divine Beast Vah Ruta and is known for his unwavering optimism.
