Meet the Faces of Hyrule

Discover my top favorite heroes and foes of Breath of the Wild

Link

The hero of Hyrule, Link embarks on a journey to defeat Calamity Ganon.

Zelda

The princess of Hyrule, burdened with the task of sealing away Ganon.

Mipha

Mipha wields the power of healing through her special ability, Mipha’s Grace.

Hestu

A giant Korok with a love for dancing and maracas.

Revali

A skilled Rito warrior with unmatched archery skills.

Sidon

The enthusiastic and courageous prince of the Zora.

Calamity Ganon

The terrifying force of ancient malice that threatens all of Hyrule. Calamity Ganon is the final challenge that tests every weapon, every skill, and every ounce of courage you’ve gathered on your journey.

Lynel

Weak to headshots and Flurry Rush. Use perfect dodges and stun with arrows for an advantage.

Molduga

Weak to bombs. Throw a bomb and detonate it when Molduga jumps for maximum damage.

Yiga Blademaster

Elite Yiga warrior who uses heavy strikes and teleportation. Lure them into attacking, then dodge to trigger a Flurry Rush.

Monk Maz Koshia

The ancient Sheikah monk who serves as the final trial. Weak to precise Flurry Rushes during his teleport attacks—dodge and counter swiftly.
