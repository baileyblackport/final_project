Meet the Faces of Hyrule

Discover my top favorite heroes and foes of Breath of the Wild

Link

The hero of Hyrule, Link embarks on a journey to defeat Calamity Ganon.

Zelda

The princess of Hyrule, burdened with the task of sealing away Ganon.

Revali

A skilled Rito warrior with unmatched archery skills.

Hestu

A giant Korok with a love for dancing and maracas.

Impa

The elder of Kakariko Village and a key figure in the Sheikah tribe.

Sidon

The enthusiastic and courageous prince of the Zora.

Lynel

Weak to headshots and Flurry Rush. Use perfect dodges and stun with arrows for an advantage.

Molduga

Weak to bombs. Throw a bomb and detonate it when Molduga jumps for maximum damage.

Vah Medoh

The Divine Beast of the skies. Weak to electricity—use arrows to hit weak points.

Vah Rudania

A fiery mechanical beast built by the Sheikah. Weak to ice—use ice-based attacks to freeze and target weak spots.
