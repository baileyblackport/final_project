The Best Weapons in Hyrule

From legendary swords to powerful bows, discover the weapons that will aid you in your quest to defeat Calamity Ganon!

Melee Weapons

Master Sword

Attack Power: 30 (60 against Ganon)

The legendary sword of Hyrule, the Master Sword, is a must-have for Link in the final battle against Calamity Ganon.

Royal Claymore Sword

Attack Power: 52

A massive two-handed sword once wielded by the knights of Hyrule. It boasts high attack power and a regal design, making it both deadly and distinguished.

Biggoron’s Sword

Attack Power: 60

A two-handed weapon with incredible damage output, perfect for taking down tough enemies.

Bows & Ranged

Great Eagle Bow

Attack Power: 28 (or 48 with full pull)

This bow is known for its long-range shots and is especially useful for aerial attacks.

Ancient Bow

Attack Power: 44

A powerful bow that shoots multiple arrows at once, effective against Guardians.

Royal Bow

Attack Power: 38

A bow favored by Hyrule's elite guards. Its fine craftsmanship and balanced build allow for precise shots with high damage—perfect for skilled archers.
